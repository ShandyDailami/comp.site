Welcome to Composite

Composite is an organization based around students’ development. We provide education with a focus of high stakes, high quality competitions to challenge youths in improving themselves.

Purple

Creativity, power and exploration. Creativity paves the way for innovation and new discoveries, allowing us to see the world from a different perspective. Power gives us the ability to translate those ideas into concrete actions and influence change. Meanwhile, exploration encourages us to explore the unknown and face new challenges. These three elements—creativity, power, and exploration—work together to create progress and expand the boundaries of our potential.
